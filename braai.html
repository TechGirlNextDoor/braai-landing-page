What is a braai?



The word braai is derived from the Afrikaans language, where it simply means barbecue or grill.
However, the practice of cooking meat over an open flame has ancient roots in South Africa.
Braaing is a tradition that continues to tie the nation together.
It is one of the few things that are not specific to one cultural or ethnic group,
in South Africa-everyone braais.
